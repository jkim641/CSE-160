Notes to the Grader:

I created a candy blocky world! Most help came from the lab, professor tutorial videos, LLM for both coding and image generation, and previous assignment code.

This project includes:
  • Interactive candy world
  • Cake crafting minigame
  • Custom textures
  • Different blocks to place
  • Music reward system
  • Block placement system
  • You can inspect and go to console to check how many cake blocks are placed on the steel pan!
Controls:
  • W-A-S-D to move around
  • Q and E to rotate camera
  • Click and hold to rotate view
  • Press F to place down blocks
  • Press G to delete blocks
  • 1 ~ 4 to choose different blocks
