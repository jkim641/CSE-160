Three.js Project

For Assignment 5, I created a Ice Cream Shop! I used a lot of helps from the three.js tutorial website, previous class assignments, and LLM. Addtionally, most of the obj models used in this project are from CGTrader and Free3D. All citation / refrences are in my README.md file(included in zip file and github).

Controls and Perspective Project.

  • W-A-S-D : Move Around
  • Mouse Drag - Look Around
  • Mouse Wheel - Zoom

Features and WOW Point

  • Handmade Ice Cream Shop with more then 20 shapes.
  • Used primary shapes: Cube, Cone, Cylinder, Sphere, Alphabet.
  • Imported OBJ Models with MTL.
  • Many custom Textures.
  • Lightnings: Directional, Ambient, Hemisphere, Point, and Spot.
  • Animated Ice Cream Scoops and fireflies around.
  • Interactive Exploration: Click between the strawberry and chocolate!
