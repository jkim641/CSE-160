Project Info 🎮

For this assignment, I used my Assignment 2: Blocky Animal to test lighting and shading effects. Most helps were from lab and professor's tutorial video, LLM, and used codes from Assignment 2 and Assignment 3.

Controls 🎹

W A S D → Move Camera
Q / E → Rotate Camera
Mouse Drag → Camera Rotation
Shift + Click → Special Animation

Features 🌟

• Normal Visualization ON/OFF
• Main Point Light ON/OFF (Yellow small cube)
• Spotlight ON/OFF (Blue small cube)
• Light Position (For the Main Light)
• Light Colors (For the Main Light)
• Camera Movement & Mouse Rotation
• Animated Animal and Main Light
• OBJ Model
